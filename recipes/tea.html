Tea Recipe

Who doesn't like tea?
I learnt this before I could learn to speak well.

co-signed!

    Ingredients

  • Water
  • Tea bag/leaves
  • Sugar
  • Milk

    Steps

  1. Boil Water
  2. Add tea bag/leaves and leave for a while.
  3. Add sugar (optional)
  4. Add milk (optional)
  5. Pour into a cup and enjoy!
